Memorial Arena

Captains face off against challenging bosses from previous encounters. The virtual battlefield uses data to recreate enemies and have them battle with Valkyries with the aim of increasing Valkyries' battle prowess. Memorial Arena runs from Tuesday, 04:00 to Monday, 02:00 every week. Between Monday 02:00 and Tuesday 04:00, results are calculated and rewards are distributed.
